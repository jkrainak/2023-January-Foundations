About Interface Web School

Our Story

Interface Web School is located in Omaha, Nebraska, and has been teaching web technology courses live and in-person since 2014. As a startup, we were free to develop our personal style of instruction that emphasizes the practical topics and experiences to help foster a deeper understanding of tech that goes beyond just the syntax.

In 2017 Inteface was acquired by the AIM Institute. AIM's mission is to grow, connect and inspire the tech talent community through educational and career development initiatives. Our focus is to get people excited about technology and inspire them to pursue a tech career - which leads to a better work force and ultimately allows communties to thrive.

FAQ

Courses

Do I need experience?
For the Basics of Web Development course, no - this course will teach you everything you need to know. For the Mobile Web Development course, yes - you should have a solid understanding of both HTML & CSS, including using advanced selectors like nth-of-type and :target.
Do I need to install any software?
You only need Chrome - everything will be presented online, and you will use our online development platform for all coding work.
Is this course hard?
Maybe - kind depends on you! The material is presented in a very step-by-step manor as it guides you through new topics while you are developing a real website. Remember that these are also self-paced courses, so if you need to review a section multiple times to really "get it", you can.
How long is the course?
Courses are open from the first of the month for 31 days, and each course is divided into about 8-10 sections, so you can typically have about 3 days on average to complete each section. Each section can take anywhere from 1-4 hours to complete.
